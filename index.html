Pendulum

The smart insole for seniors

    Falls are the main cause of death from injury among people over the age of 65

  • Every year 1/3 of all people over 65 are suffering from falls.

  • Older adults who fall once are two to three times as likely to fall again within a year

  • Every 11 seconds, an older adult is treated in the emergency room for a fall

  • Fall prevention strategies should include the promotion of engineering to remove the potential for falls and the education of individuals and communities to build risk awareness.

    • From World Health Organization

About Pendulum

Mission
What?

We believe we can prevent and predict the risk of falling and fragility in the elderly generation through the combination of wearable technology and machine learning

Value
How?

Our connected insole allows senior adults and their relatives to track their gait characteristics and generate data-driven recommendations on simple but effective exercises targeted at preventing the risk of falling
